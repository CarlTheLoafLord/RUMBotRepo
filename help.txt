c?help
Displays this list.
c?servers
Displays servers that Cranberry is in
c?info
Displays bot info.
c?coinflip or cf
Flips a coin.
c?bubblewrap or bw
Makes a bubblewrap with text or emote
c?test
Sends the bot instance IDs
c?rockpaperscissors or rps
Play Rock Paper Scissors against another user or the bot
c?connect4 or c4
Play Connect 4 against another user
